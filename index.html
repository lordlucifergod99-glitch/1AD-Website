Rank Structure

The 1AD follows a structured ranking system divided into Command, Staff, and Enlisted. Each rank signifies growing responsibility, leadership, and dedication.

Command

  • [UCO] Unit Commanding Officer
  • [UXO] Unit Executive Officer
  • [UCPT] Unit Captain

Staff

  • [CFO] Chief Officer
  • [SO] Senior Officer
  • [OFF] Officer
  • [JO] Junior Officer

Enlisted

  • [C.OP] Chief Operator
  • [S.OP] Senior Operator
  • [OP] Operator
  • [CMBT] Combatant
  • [REC] Recruit
